HILI FUN CITY

 

This flagship family entertainment destination in Al Ain has been a firm favorite since opening in 1985 when it became the Gulf's first theme park.

Newly renovated, the park boasts 28 attractions for all ages, a family show Amphitheatre, picnic and play areas. The all-inclusive price of AED 50 (US$ 13.61) Wednesday to Saturday and AED 45 (US$ 12.25) Mondays and Tuesdays covers entry to the park and 25 attractions. Admission is free for children of 0.89 meters (35 inches) and below.

Opening Times: 

  October-May June - September
Public Holidays 10am - 10pm 4pm - 10pm
Friday & Saturday 12pm - 10pm 4pm - 10pm
Monday -Thursday 4pm - 10pm 5pm - 10pm
Wednesdays are reserved for ladies only and the park is closed on Sundays (unless a public holiday falls on Sunday).
